Learn

Participate in HackWeek, the whole week leading up to the hackathon the cosponsoring clubs and organizations will be putting on a variety of workshops to help get you up to speed on popular hardware and software. No experience necessary! You can attend as many or as few of them as you want, detailed schedule will be coming soon...

settings

Hack

Work on turning your project from idea into reality in a weekend long hackathon. We provide food, snacks, drinks, hardware, and mentors; everything you need to stay focused and have fun for the whole event, free for all participants! Additionally, thanks to the Collablab, for the majority of the event you will have access to the on campus maker space.

group

Compete

Present your project at the end of the hackathon to our panel of judges, and win various prizes! Grand prizes are available for the top three teams, the best hardware project, and the best Microsoft project. Additionally, smaller prizes will also be available for various categories. Finally, throughout the whole event we will be having small games and raffles for additional swag and small prizes!
